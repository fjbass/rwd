What is VIA University College?

VIA University College is one of Denmark's six professional colleges. The vocational colleges offer most of the country's medium-term higher education - also called professional bachelor programs. In the Central Jutland Region, the professional educations are gathered in VIA University College. We train teachers, educators, nurses, engineers, physiotherapists, designers and many more.
